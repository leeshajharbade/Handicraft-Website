About us

Crafted

A premium interior decor brand like no other, Crafted combines luxury with authenticity. Designed in-house, our exclusive range of ethically handcrafted products are shaped by expert artisans over months of engagement with age-old materials and processes. Each product in the Crafted range is a reflection of ancient tradition with a modern twist, striving to revive traditional craft through contemporary aesthetic.

Our Story

Crafted comes from a place and time forgotten. Our story is one of revival and reconnection to craft. Get connected to the Crafted way of life through a journey into the Crafted story – how we came to be, our memories, dreams, ambitions and ensemble of what our brand stands for.
